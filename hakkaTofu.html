Tofu-Faan

“Tofu-faan is very famous! You and your brother loved it a lot!

It's made out of mushroom and then we mince it with pork and crispy

it and then add tofu cubes. We then fry everything! ”

Ingredients

Minced Pork (mix all afterwards)

  • 1 handful of minced pork
  • 1 teaspoon of seasoning soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sweet soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca starch
  • 1 teaspoon of white pepper
  • ½ bowl of soup (homemade or canned)

Vegetables

  • 2 handfuls of shiitake mushrooms
  • Bowl of hot water
  • Handful of salt
  • Minced spring onions
  • 2 packs of egg tofu and cut into small pieces

Final Sauce (combine in one bowl)

  • 3 tablespoons of tapioca starch
  • 1 tablespoon of sweet soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon of oyster sauce

Method

Preparing Shiitake Mushrooms

  1. Get 2 handfuls of shiitake mushrooms and soak in very hot water for 4 hours.
  2. Cut off their stems afterwards.
  3. Take salt and squeeze to wash them. Rinse to squeeze water out.
  4. Put them into blender and pulse it 5-6 times.

Before adding Pork and Mushrooms

  1. Mix raw minced pork with its sauce with hands.
  2. Add half-bowl of soup (homemade or canned).
  3. Get a large wok and add ¼ cup of oil using a ladle on high heat.
  4. Put chopsticks in oil and see if bubbles form around it.

Cooking Pork and Mushrooms

  1. Add all meat and mushrooms into wok after seeing bubble form in oil.
  2. Mince mixture with silicon spatula and fold (every 2-3 seconds).
  3. Add chopped tofu once meat is light brown.
  4. Put ½ bowl of soup into the wok and let it melt (if refrigerated).
  5. When mixture starts bubbling, add sauce and SLOWLY mix (do not add all at once).
  6. Add boiling water if too thick. Lastly add minced spring onions and you’re done! YAY!
