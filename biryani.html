Ingredients

Rice and Sauce

  • 6 cups of Basmati rice
  • 1 packet of 2 Spices (Korma & Sindhi biryani)
  • 1/2 cup Cow ghee
  • 1/2 corn oil

Chicken and Vegetables

  • 20 pieces of chicken legs
  • Chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of tamarind chutney sauce
  • 5 big onions (cut into this strips)
  • 3 big ground tomatoes
  • One large cauliflower

Final Sauce (combine in one bowl)

  • Cinnamon bay leaf, cloves ect
  • 1 cup of chicken broth

Rice Yogurt

  • 1 tablespoon lime yogurt (makes rice fluffier)
  • Add 1 pinch of saffron
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Method

Sweet Potatoes

  1. Cover large pan with corn oil on high heat.
  2. Put chopsticks in oil and see if bubbles form around it.
  3. Submerge sweet potatoes. Wait until they are brown and flip using silicon spatula, alternate putting potatoes in the middle of pan.
  4. Put everything in a large strainer on top of large bowl. Use mini strainer to remove the potato bits from large pan.

Cooking Chicken

  1. Mixed ingredients with chicken and mix.
  2. Use previously used pan oil from the Sweet Potatoes and wait till chicken becomes light brown.
  3. Flip chicken (place outer chicken into the middle of pan to cook them evenly).
  4. Take light brown chicken out and repeat process until you have no chicken left.

Chicken and Veggies with Sauce

  1. Strain oil from chicken and use ladle to put some oil on the pan.
  2. Add veggies and mix them. Add final sauce and shut heat off.
  3. Pour fried chicken afterwards and mix everything. Make sure to dig underneath so they do not burn!
  4. Move pan away from stove-top and mix everything.
  5. Add everything to large bowl or glass container and you’re done! YAY!
